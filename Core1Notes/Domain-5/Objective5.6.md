Objective 5.6 - Troubleshoot Printer Issues

Common Symptoms

Lines Down the Printed Pages

Definition: Vertical or horizontal lines appearing repeatedly on printed documents.
Example: A damaged drum causing black lines on every page.

Garbled Print

Definition: Printed output containing unreadable or random characters.
Example: An incorrect printer driver producing meaningless symbols.

Paper Jams

Definition: Paper becoming stuck inside the printer during operation.
Example: A torn sheet blocking the paper path.

Faded Prints

Definition: Printed output appearing lighter than expected.
Example: A low toner cartridge producing faint text.

Paper Not Feeding

Definition: The printer fails to pull paper from the tray.
Example: Worn feed rollers preventing paper pickup.

Multipage Misfeed

Definition: Multiple sheets being fed through the printer at the same time.
Example: Dirty separation pads causing several pages to feed together.

Multiple Prints Pending in Queue

Definition: Print jobs accumulating without being processed.
Example: A printer paused while jobs continue to enter the queue.

Speckling on Printed Pages

Definition: Small dots or marks appearing randomly on printed output.
Example: Toner debris causing spots across the page.

Double/Echo Images on the Print

Definition: Faint duplicate images appearing on the page.
Example: A failing fuser causing ghosted text.

Grinding Noise

Definition: Unusual mechanical sounds during printer operation.
Example: A damaged gear producing grinding sounds.

Finishing Issues

Staple Jams

Definition: A blockage occurring within the printer's stapling mechanism.
Example: A bent staple preventing the finisher from operating.

Hole Punch Issues

Definition: Problems with the printer's hole-punch finishing feature.
Example: Paper scraps blocking the hole-punch mechanism.

---

Other Printer Issues

Incorrect Page Orientation

Definition: Pages printing in the wrong portrait or landscape format.
Example: A spreadsheet printing in portrait instead of landscape mode.

Tray Not Recognized

Definition: The printer fails to detect an installed paper tray.
Example: A tray not fully seated in the printer.

Connectivity Issues

Definition: Problems preventing communication between devices and the printer.
Example: A disconnected network cable causing printing failures.

Frozen Print Queue

Definition: Print jobs becoming stuck and preventing new jobs from printing.
Example: A corrupted print job blocking all subsequent print jobs.
