Objective 5.3 - Troubleshoot Video, Projector, and Display Issues

Common Symptoms

Incorrect Input Source

Definition: The display is set to the wrong input connection.
Example: A monitor set to HDMI when the computer is connected through DisplayPort.

Physical Cabling Issues

Definition: Problems caused by damaged, loose, or disconnected video cables.
Example: A loose HDMI cable causing no display output.

Burnt-Out Bulb

Definition: A failed projector lamp that no longer produces light.
Example: A projector displaying a black screen due to a dead bulb.

Fuzzy Image

Definition: A display image that appears blurry or unclear.
Example: A projector showing a blurry presentation because it is out of focus.

Display Burn-In

Definition: A permanent image ghosting effect caused by static content remaining on screen for extended periods.
Example: A faint taskbar remaining visible on an OLED display.

Dead Pixels

Definition: Individual screen pixels that remain permanently off.
Example: Small black dots visible on a monitor screen.

Flashing Screen

Definition: A display that flickers or repeatedly turns on and off.
Example: A faulty display cable causing screen flickering.

Incorrect Color Display

Definition: Colors appearing inaccurate or distorted on a display.
Example: A monitor showing a blue tint because of a loose video connector.

Audio Issues

Definition: Problems with sound output through a monitor or projector.
Example: No audio over HDMI despite video working properly.

Dim Image

Definition: A display image that appears unusually dark.
Example: A projector bulb nearing the end of its lifespan.

Intermittent Projector Shutdown

Definition: A projector randomly powering off during operation.
Example: A projector shutting down because of overheating.

Sizing Issues

Definition: Display content appearing too large, too small, or improperly scaled.
Example: A monitor displaying a lower resolution than its native resolution.

Distorted Image

Definition: An image that appears stretched, warped, or misshapen.
Example: Incorrect aspect ratio settings causing a stretched display.
