# Objective 4.10 - Basic Concepts Related to Artificial Intelligence (AI)

# Application Integration

### Application Integration

**Definition:** The process of connecting AI tools with existing applications and services.
**Example:** Integrating an AI chatbot into a customer support system.

---

# Policy

### Appropriate Use

**Definition:** Using AI systems in accordance with organizational rules and guidelines.
**Example:** Using AI to draft emails but not to make unauthorized business decisions.

### Plagiarism

**Definition:** Presenting AI-generated or copied content as original work without proper attribution.
**Example:** Submitting AI-generated text as a school assignment without citing the source.

---

# Limitations

### Bias

**Definition:** The tendency of an AI system to produce unfair or skewed results based on its training data.
**Example:** An AI hiring tool favoring certain candidates due to biased training data.

### Hallucinations

**Definition:** Incorrect or fabricated information generated by an AI system.
**Example:** An AI providing a nonexistent website as a reference.

### Accuracy

**Definition:** The degree to which AI-generated information is correct and reliable.
**Example:** Verifying AI-generated troubleshooting steps before applying them.

---

# Private vs. Public AI

### Private AI

**Definition:** An AI system that operates using restricted data within an organization.
**Example:** A company-hosted AI assistant used only by employees.

### Public AI

**Definition:** An AI system available to the general public over the internet.
**Example:** A publicly accessible AI chatbot service.

---

# Data Security

### Data Security

**Definition:** Protecting information from unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure.
**Example:** Preventing confidential company documents from being uploaded to public AI tools.

### Data Source

**Definition:** The origin of information used to train or provide input to an AI system.
**Example:** An AI model trained using company knowledge-base articles.

### Data Privacy

**Definition:** Protecting personal and sensitive information from unauthorized use or exposure.
**Example:** Removing customer personal information before submitting data to an AI system.
